About Me

Hi, I am Matthieu.
A 23 year young guy from the Netherlands, currently based in Kampen.
I have recently graduated from the BUAS (formerly NHTV) in the study of Creative Media and Game Technology (formerly Game Architecture and Design) as a Game Programmer.

what I do

I'm a game programmer with interest in GamePlay and Artificial Intelligence systems.

I have a wide skillset with multiple engines and languages, such as Unity (C#) and Unreal engine(C++),
multiple types of version control, for example GitHub and svn,
and other development softwares as slack, jenkins, and trello.

My experience with soft skills include:
Fluent work in english
Teamwork in a team of 10+
Working under pressure

Besides programming, in my free time I like to solve (logic)puzzles, ride on my bike, and take pictures.
